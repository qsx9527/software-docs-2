# Introduction

AIBOX-8550 features the Qualcomm QCS8550 octa-core AI processor with an integrated 48 TOPS NPU, supporting mainstream AI models and deep learning frameworks. The built-in Adreno 740 GPU enables ray tracing and 8K video codec. Housed in an industrial-grade all-metal casing for efficient heat dissipation, it ensures 24/7 stable operation to meet rigorous industrial demands.
