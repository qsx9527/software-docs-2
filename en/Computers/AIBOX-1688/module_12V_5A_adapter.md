# 12V Power Adapter

## Product Parameters

* Product: Power Adapter (Desktop Type)
* Specification: US/European Standard
* Input Standard: AC100-240V 50/60Hz
* Output Standard: 12V-5A

**Note:** The AIBOX-1688 all-in-one machine requires a power supply of 12V/5A for normal operation. If the current is less than 5A, it may restart abnormally due to insufficient current. To ensure the normal operation of the development board, please use a power supply with a voltage of 12V and a current of 5A. It is recommended to use the power accessories from the Firefly official website.
