# 12V Power Adapter

## Product Parameters

* Product: Power Adapter
* Specification: US/EU Standard
* Input Standard: AC110-240V 50/60Hz
* Output Standard: 12V-2A

**Note:** The AIBOX-1688 all-in-one machine requires a power supply of 12V/2A for normal operation. A current lower than 2A may cause abnormal restarts due to insufficient current. To ensure the normal operation of the development board, please use a power supply with a voltage of 12V and a current of 2A~3A. It is recommended to use power accessories from the Firefly official website.
