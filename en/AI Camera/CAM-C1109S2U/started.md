## Introduction

**CAM-C1109S2U** uses the 14nm low-power AI vision processor RV1109, with dual-core 32-bit ARM Cortex-A7 architecture, integrated NEON and FPU, main frequency up to 1.5GHz, can achieve FastBoot fast startup, and supports TrustZone technology and multiple cryptographic engines. Equipped with a quad-core high-performance AI intelligent vision processor, the computing power is up to 1.2Tops, supports H.264/H.265 video codec, multiple AI frameworks and liveness detection, with high recognition accuracy, it can be applied to face recognition, gate access control, gesture recognition, expression recognition, face attribute analysis and other scenarios; open supporting materials, easy to develop, help the project to land quickly. Equipped with an all-aluminum alloy shell, efficient heat dissipation, ultra-small size, space saving, can be flexibly embedded in various AI products.
