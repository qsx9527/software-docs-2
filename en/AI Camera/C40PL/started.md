## Introduction

**C40PL** is equipped with a quad-core high-performance AI intelligent vision processor with a computing power of up to 2.0Tops. It supports 4K video encoding and decoding, multiple AI frameworks and liveness detection, has high recognition accuracy, and is equipped with a 400W HDR high dynamic infrared enhanced wide-angle camera. It is suitable for scenarios such as face recognition and license plate recognition.

![](../../../rv1126_img/C40PL/c40pl.jpg)

**Core-1126-JD4** adopts Rockchip RV1126 series processor, with built-in AI neural network acceleration NPU computing power up to 2.0Tops, supports 4K H.265/H.264 multi-channel encoding and decoding, built-in 14 million ISP 2.0, with multi-level noise reduction, 3-frame HDR and other technologies, supports 3 cameras input at the same time, meets the industry needs of security products and AIoT, and is widely used in: face recognition, IPC smart network cameras, gate access control, smart security, smart finance/construction sites, smart travel and other industries.


![](../../../rv1126_img/Core-1126-JD4/Core-1126-JD4-front.png)  
      

The [AIO-1126JD4](https://item.taobao.com/item.htm?id=670519903322) development board consists of the core board Core-1126-JD4 + the base board MB-JD4-RV11091126. AIO-1126JD4 has rich interfaces such as RGMII, USB2.0, I2C, UART, GPIO, MIPI-DSI and MIPI-CSI, which can be directly applied to various smart products to accelerate product implementation. For details, please refer to [Interface Definition](interface_definition.md).
