### Product parameter

* Product parameter：Power Adapter
* Specifications: EU Standard／US Standard
* Input：AC110-240V 50/60Hz
* Output：12V-2A

<font color=#ff0000>Note: AIO-3399C normal work requires power 12V/2A, it probably abnormal restart if the current is less than 2A. In order to ensure the normal operation of the AIO-3399C, please use the voltage of 12V, current is 2A ~ 3A power, and we suggest to use the official power.</font>
