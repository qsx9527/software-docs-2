## Product parameters

* Product: Power Adapter
* Specifications: US/EU
* Input standard: AC110-240V 50/60Hz
* Output standard: 12V-2A

**Note:** The AIO-1684XQ all-in-one machine needs a power supply of 12V/2A for normal operation. If the current is lower than 2A, it may restart abnormally due to too small current. In order to ensure the normal operation of the development board, please use a voltage of 12V and a current of 12V. For the 2A~3A power supply, it is recommended to use the Firefly official website power supply accessories.
